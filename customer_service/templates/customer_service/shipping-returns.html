Shipping & Returns

We strive to deliver your favorite books to your doorstep with the utmost care. Here’s everything you need to know about our shipping policies and return procedures.

Shipping Information

All orders are processed within 2-3 business days. Orders are not shipped or delivered on weekends or holidays. If we are experiencing a high volume of orders, shipments may be delayed by a few days. You will receive a confirmation email once your order has been shipped, including a tracking number for your convenience.

Free Shipping Over €50

Enjoy free standard shipping on all domestic orders over €50. No promo code needed—the discount will be applied automatically at checkout. This offer is only valid within EU countries.

International Shipping

We also ship worldwide! Please note that international orders may be subject to additional customs fees or taxes imposed by the destination country, which are the responsibility of the customer.

Shipping Time: International deliveries typically take between 7-14 business days, but may be delayed due to customs clearance or other factors beyond our control.

Return Policy

Your satisfaction is our priority. If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, you can return the product within 30 days of receipt for a full refund or exchange, provided the item is in its original condition. To initiate a return, please contact us at support@teacuptales.com with your order number and reason for return.

Conditions for Returns:

  • Items must be returned in their original condition (unopened, unused, with all original packaging).
  • Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the product is defective or you received the wrong item.
  • Refunds will be issued to your original payment method within 7-10 business days after we receive the returned item.

If you have any questions regarding our shipping or return policies, feel free to reach out to us at teacuptalesbooks@gmail.com. We're happy to assist!

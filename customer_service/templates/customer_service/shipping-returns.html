Shipping & Returns

We strive to deliver your favorite books to your doorstep with the utmost care. Here’s everything you need to know about our shipping policies and return procedures.

Shipping Information

All orders are processed within 2-3 business days. Orders are not shipped or delivered on weekends or holidays. If we are experiencing a high volume of orders, shipments may be delayed by a few days.

Domestic Shipping

We offer standard and express shipping across the country. Shipping costs depend on your order’s size and location.

International Shipping

We ship worldwide, with rates calculated at checkout. Please note that international deliveries may take longer due to customs processes.

Return Policy

If you are not satisfied with your purchase, you can return the product within 30 days of receipt for a full refund or exchange.

For more information, please contact our support team at support@teacuptales.com.
